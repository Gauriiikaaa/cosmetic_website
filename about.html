About Gbeauty

Welcome to Gbeauty, your ultimate destination for high-quality cosmetic products. At gbeauty, we take pride in providing a curated collection of beauty and skincare products to help you look and feel your best.

We believe in supporting small businesses and empowering entrepreneurs in the beauty industry. Our platform connects customers with a wide range of small beauty businesses, offering unique and artisanal products that you won't find anywhere else.

About the Owner - Gaurika Sawhney

Hi, I'm Gaurika Sawhney, the proud founder and owner of gbeauty. With a passion for cosmetics and a vision to support small businesses, I started this venture to create a platform where both customers and entrepreneurs can thrive.

Having worked in the beauty industry for over a decade, I understand the value of high-quality products that enhance your natural beauty. I carefully select each product in our collection to ensure it meets the highest standards of quality and safety.
